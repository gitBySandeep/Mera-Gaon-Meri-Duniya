About MGMD

We are working in the sector of Youth Development and Education transformation at the grassroots. Dedicated and invested youth can transform a village is the foundational idea Mera Gaon Meri Dunia stands on. We believe that youth with the best mind and intent in dedicated time can bring considerable change in the community. Our core focus is transforming education scenarios in the community through local youth leaders.

Aaina Dekho

The focus of the current education system is on academic result and not on the holistic development of the children. Also, due to ever increasing diversity and existence of huge learning gaps among children in a classroom, we are approaching a complete breakdown of the current education system.

Sparsh initiative was marked by rural youth-led community classrooms for bridging gaps in age-apt learning outcome of government primary school children and creating a learning conducive environment for children of the village. We believe that this is an efficient way to add contextuality in education for children.

Aaina Dekho is a 6-day residential program for youth leadership journey. The program marks the start of a journey towards realizing the attributes, values and skills of ethical leadership. Our participants navigate together through this journey by actively experiencing a series of workshops and spaces thoughtfully designed to discover themselves.
